## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- **Who:** Lawyers in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, and broader India
- **Firm size:** Solo practitioners and small firms (2-10 lawyers)
- **Language:** English is second language — keep copy simple, visual-first
- **Pain points:** Manual case timeline creation, scattered documents, slow drafting
- **Motivation:** Compete with larger firms, save time, look professional to clients
