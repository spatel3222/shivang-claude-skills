# Tools by Channel

Current as of early 2025. Always verify pricing and features before recommending — this space moves fast.

## Cold Email

| Tool | Best For | Price Tier | Notes |
|------|----------|------------|-------|
| **Apollo.io** | Prospecting + sequences | Mid | Strong data, built-in sequences. Good all-in-one for early stage. |
| **Instantly.ai** | High-volume cold email | Low-Mid | Great deliverability tools, unlimited email accounts. Pair with Apollo for data. |
| **Lemlist** | Personalized outreach | Mid | Image/video personalization. Good for standing out in crowded inboxes. |
| **Smartlead** | Agency-scale outreach | Mid | Multi-client, white-label. Overkill for single-product startups. |
| **Clay** | Data enrichment + workflows | Mid-High | Powerful for building enriched prospect lists. Connects to everything. |
| **Warmbox / Mailreach** | Email warmup | Low | Essential for new domains. Don't skip this step. |

**Compliance:** CAN-SPAM requires opt-out link, physical address, no deceptive subjects. GDPR applies to EU prospects — legitimate interest can work for B2B but document your reasoning. Don't buy scraped personal email lists.

**Deliverability stack:** Separate domains for outreach (never send cold email from your main domain). Warm up for 2-3 weeks minimum. Keep volume under 50/day per inbox initially.

## SMS

| Tool | Best For | Price Tier | Notes |
|------|----------|------------|-------|
| **Twilio** | Developer-first SMS/MMS | Pay-per-use | Most flexible. Requires dev work. |
| **Postscript** | E-commerce SMS | Mid | Shopify-native. Not great for SaaS. |
| **Attentive** | Enterprise SMS marketing | High | Best-in-class for large consumer brands. |
| **SlickText** | Simple SMS campaigns | Low-Mid | Good for getting started without dev resources. |

**Compliance:** TCPA requires express written consent before texting. Fines are $500-$1,500 PER MESSAGE. This is the strictest channel — do not cut corners. Always use double opt-in. Include opt-out in every message.

## Instagram

| Tool | Best For | Price Tier | Notes |
|------|----------|------------|-------|
| **ManyChat** | DM automation + comment triggers + lead capture | Low-Mid | The engine behind "Comment X to get Y" flows. Captures email + phone, tags leads, integrates with CRM. This is the #1 Instagram monetization tool — not optional if you're serious about revenue from IG. |
| **Instagram Edits** | Native reel editing | Free | Edit reels inside Instagram's own app. Algorithm favors natively-edited content over imported. Pro tip worth knowing. |
| **Later / Buffer** | Scheduling + analytics | Low | Basic posting. Not a growth tool by itself. |
| **Flick** | Hashtag research + analytics | Low | Useful for discovery strategy. |
| **Meta Ads Manager** | Paid amplification | Variable | Required for any paid Instagram strategy. |
| **Cosmos / Pinterest** | Brand mood board + visual inspiration | Free | Curate design references, build brand aesthetic boards. Useful for defining visual identity before creating content. |
| **Figma** | Grid planning + design system | Free-Mid | Plan 9-post grid previews, maintain carousel templates, ensure visual consistency. |

**Instagram Growth System:**
- **Carousels are king** — 8 slides, minimal text per slide, number-driven hooks ("8 traits...", "10 systems..."), shareable individual slides. Carousels consistently outperform all other formats.
- **50/50 split** — ~50% carousels, ~50% reels. Carousels drive saves and shares. Reels drive reach and discovery.
- **ManyChat CTA on every post** — Every carousel ends with a CTA: "Comment [KEYWORD] to get [lead magnet]." This bridges content → leads → revenue.
- **Lead magnets = revenue engine** — Google Docs/Slides as free resources. Simple but powerful. Can drive 80% of revenue when combined with proper CRM tagging.
- **Grid intentionality** — Light/dark alternating pattern, consistent brand colors (2-3 max), consistent font. 20% profile-to-follow rate (vs. 1% average) comes from visual consistency. Every 9 posts should tell a complete story.
- **Edit reels natively** — Use Instagram's Edits app. Algorithm promotes natively-edited content over imports from CapCut/Premiere.

**Compliance:** Instagram TOS prohibits automated follows/unfollows/likes (RIP growth hacking bots). Comment automation via ManyChat is compliant if triggered by user action. Don't buy followers — it kills algorithmic reach.

## LinkedIn

| Tool | Best For | Price Tier | Notes |
|------|----------|------------|-------|
| **Apollo.io** | Prospecting + finding contacts | Mid | Already in your stack. Use for LinkedIn prospect identification. |
| **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** | Advanced search + lead lists | High | Worth it if LinkedIn is a primary channel. InMail credits included. |
| **Dripify / Expandi** | LinkedIn automation | Mid | Automate connection requests + follow-ups. Use carefully — LinkedIn bans accounts. |
| **Shield** | Profile + content analytics | Low | Track what content performs. |
| **Taplio** | Content + engagement | Mid | AI-assisted LinkedIn content strategy. |

**Compliance:** LinkedIn restricts automation tools and can ban accounts. Keep connection requests under 100/week. Personalize every request. Don't use automation on your primary account — if you must automate, use a secondary account you can afford to lose. LinkedIn's algorithm heavily favors native content over links.
